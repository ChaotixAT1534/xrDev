In "Worlds Unfolding" edited by Amy Xiaofan Jiang, she says that virtual worlds can be engaging if you include creativity and insert realities we inhabit. World Making is defined as creating or reshaping a world by transforming environments. Includes storytelling, cultural, political, technological, and ecological contexts. Virtual space can be defined as websites, chatrooms, online platforms, gaming environment, the metaverse. This reading helped me a lot to think of ways how to world build in a virtual space.
