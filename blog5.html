In Hamlet on the Holodeck the author argues that computers offers many possibilities for storytelling. It adapts from older tradition of storytelling but also a new expressive way. Such as having the ability to interact with storytelling. She talks about the important affordance in digital design like prodecural design and participatory design. The other two discussed were encyclopedic extent and coherent spatial navigation. It means interactivity and immersion. She then discusses how additive form is necessary for expressing more media formats and genres. An example of that is "ebook."
